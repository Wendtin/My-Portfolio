SESSION 14: GROUP POLICY AUDIT REPORT

Question 1: What specific command do you run on a client machine to force it to download the lastest Group Policy from the Domain controller?
gpupdate /force is the command that we run on the Client machine to force it to download the latest Group Policy from the Domain Controller.

Question 2: Explain the LSDOU acronym. If a Local Policy says "Enable USB" but the Domain Policy says "Disable USB", which one wins and why?
LSDOU stands for Local, Site, Domain, and Organizational Unit.
The Organizational Unit (OU) policy wins because it is the most specific and is applied last in the order of predecence.
Question 3: why is it best practice to apply GPOs to Organizational Units (OUs) rather than the entire Domain?
It's best practice to apply GPOs to OUs to ensure granular control.
This prevents locking out high-level accounts (like Administrators) while still enforcing rules on specific department (like Engineering).
